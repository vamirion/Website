Our Story

Why We Started CodeNest

Technology is becoming part of every industry and every part of society. At CodeNest, we believe every student — regardless of gender or background — deserves the chance to understand programming further, because programming is not only a valuable skill but also a way to think critically and creatively.

We started CodeNest to make programming, robotics, and AI feel approachable for primary school students. Through hands-on projects, students build games, program robots, and learn to solve meaningful challenges with confidence.

Our lessons are designed to feel fun and supportive while still developing serious thinking skills: decomposition, logic, debugging, creativity, communication, and resilience.
