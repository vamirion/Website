Our Story

Why We Started CodeNest

Children today are surrounded by technology, but many only experience it as users. CodeNest was created to help students understand what happens behind the screen — then use that understanding to build games, program robots, and solve meaningful challenges.

Our lessons are designed to feel fun and approachable while still developing serious thinking skills: decomposition, logic, debugging, creativity, communication, and confidence.
